Our History

TechCon began in 2010 as a small developer meetup and has since grown into a premier technology conference, attracting innovators, tech leaders, and enthusiasts from around the globe. Over the years, it has evolved into a hub for discussing the future of tech and unveiling breakthroughs in AI, Web3, cybersecurity, and more.

Our Mission

TechCon aims to inspire and connect the global tech community by providing a platform for education, collaboration, and innovation. We are committed to promoting diversity in tech and supporting young talent through mentorship and visibility.
